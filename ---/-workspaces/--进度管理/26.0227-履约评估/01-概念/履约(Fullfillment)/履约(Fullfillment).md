## 内容
- "Fulfill" (US) or "fulfil" (UK) means ==to carry out, complete, or satisfy a duty, promise, requirement, or desire==.

Key aspects of "fulfill" include:
- **Definitions:** To bring to reality, obey commands, satisfy needs, or finish a task.
- **Common Usage:** "Fulfill a promise," "fulfill requirements," "fulfill a dream," or "order fulfillment".
- **Spelling Variations:** "Fulfill" is standard in the U.S., while "fulfil" is common in the UK and Australia
- **Synonyms:** Complete, execute, satisfy, achieve, realize, discharge, and perform.
- **Contextual Examples:** A company fulfilling orders, a person fulfilling their potential, or a student fulfilling academic requirements.
