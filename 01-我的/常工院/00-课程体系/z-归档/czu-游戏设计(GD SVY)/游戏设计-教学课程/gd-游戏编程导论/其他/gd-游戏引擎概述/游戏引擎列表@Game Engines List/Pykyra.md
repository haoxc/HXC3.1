## About
PyKyra is one of the fastest game development frameworks for Python that is based on both [[SDL]] (Software and Documentation Localisation) and the Kyra engine. Along with the standard features of this framework, it also supports MPEG video, sound such as MP3, Ogg Vorbis, Wav, etc., direct images reading, and much more.
