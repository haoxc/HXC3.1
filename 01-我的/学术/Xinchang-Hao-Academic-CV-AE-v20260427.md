# Xinchang Hao

School of Art and Design, Changzhou Institute of Technology, China  
Email: haoxc@outlook.com

## Academic Profile

Dr. Xinchang Hao is an Assistant Professor at the School of Art and Design, Changzhou Institute of Technology. His research focuses on industrial and management engineering, systems engineering, intelligent computation, enterprise resource scheduling, multi-objective optimization, discrete system modeling and control, data governance, and process optimization for lean service management.

He worked and conducted research in Japan for 13 years, with professional experience in product planning, system architecture, large-scale information systems, prototype development, and feasibility assessment for enterprises and research organizations in manufacturing, finance, medical testing, office equipment, and information services. Since joining Prof. Mitsuo Gen's laboratory at the Graduate School of Information, Production and Systems, Waseda University in 2008, he has conducted research on intelligent algorithms for enterprise resource scheduling, resource optimization, robust scheduling, and service-oriented information system planning.

Since returning to China in 2017, Dr. Hao has been dedicated to interdisciplinary industry-education integration and academic knowledge transfer. His work supports enterprises in building strategy-oriented refined operation systems, especially for traditional-industry digital transformation, integrated R&D-production-sales management, and data governance.
